In this Game there are 3 Components named "Rock", "Paper" and "scissors". Where you get to choose one and System will generate one, winner will be decided according to rules.

  • "Rock" can beats "Scissors" but beaten by "Paper".
  • "Paper" can beat "Rock" but beaten by "Scissors"
  • "Scissors" can beat "Paper" but beaten by "Rock"
